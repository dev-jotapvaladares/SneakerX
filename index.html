In 1984, Nike unveiled the Air Jordan 1, a basketball sneaker designed by Peter Moore for a young Michael Jordan. The iconic silhouette transcended sneakers and sports, altering the trajectory of style forever. Today, Michael Jordan’s namesake brand continues to inspire sportswear and style movements around the world through new releases, retro drops and unexpected collaborations.

Balenciaga

Balenciaga Wmns Track Trainer 'Triple Black'

Christian Dior once referred to couturier Cristóbal Balenciaga as “the master of us all.” Founded in 1917 in Bilbao, Spain, today the house is based in Paris and known for its innovative approach to luxury. Since 2015, Georgian designer Demna Gvasalia has applied his irreverent streetwear sensibilities to Balenciaga’s seasonal collections as creative director. This shift is noticeable throughout the brand’s product catalog, from sneakers and apparel to accessories.

Off-White

Off-White ODSY-2000 'Black Multi'

Off-White was founded in 2012, envisioned by the late Virgil Abloh as "the grey area between black and white." The Milan-based label bridges the gap between streetwear and luxury, and is known for its stylized use of quotation marks and bold graphics. In addition to its expansive apparel collections, Off-White’s sneaker catalog includes iconic collaborations with Nike.
